About me

I am an engineering student at VJTI, Mumbai, currently pursuing a degree in Eletronics & Telecommunication engineering. I do freelance UI/UX designing, graphics designing, branding , etc.

I always had an interest in designing and arts since childhood. I started exploring graphic design and learning design related softwares in my college as a co-curricular activity. There, I learned Photoshop, Illustrator and Lightroom. I made social media posts for events and festivals for college handle. Soon, I developed an interet in UI/UX as it was a perfect blend of art of designing and little bit of coding skills in HTML and CSS. I could get best of both the worlds in one field. Since then, I have completed several courses and workshops including "Foundations of UI/UX" from Google and have been working on polishing my skills.

I beleive that the true meaning of a good User Interface is to make an interface which is accessible to all, yet brings some new features to the table for those who wish to explore more.
